Projects

  • The water storage capacity of Gibaltar Reservoir, located in Santa Barbara County, is being threatened by sedimentation from the Santa Ynez Watershed. This project examines the historical reservoir storage capacity due to sedimentation.

    Gibraltar Reservoir Capacity

    Data visualization

  • By using records of precipitation from Santa Barbara County and discharge recorded by the US Geological Survey, the historical runoff ratio can be calculated for Santa Cruz Creek in the Santa Ynez Watershed. This is helpful for modeling streamflow in the watershed.

    Runoff ratio in Santa Cruz Creek sub-basin

    water management

  • The snowshoe hare population tends to follow a 10-year cycle, according to Alaska Fish & Wildlife. This project examines the characteristics of snowshoe hares observed in Bonanza Creek, Alaska.

    Snowshoe Hare Examination

    data wrangling

  • Water samples from the Santa Ynez River can paint a picture regarding the water quality of the Santa Ynez River. Since 1998, the Santa Ynez River has been sampled for total dissolved solids and total suspended solids.

    Santa Ynez River Water Quality

    water quality
